Platform: Windows
Build: C++

Battle of Wizards is a 2D adventure roguelite, taking heavy inspiration from games such as A Link to the Past, Rogue Legacy and Enter the Gungeon. Solys ended as a vertical slice for a tech demo, featuring an event-based cutscene system, melee combat and an equipment system heavily bolstered by a roguelike-inspired random loot system.

Within the project, I took the role of developing the engine on top of SFML and developing core gameplay systems, such as events and dialogue.
